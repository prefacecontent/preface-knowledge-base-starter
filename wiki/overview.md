# Overview

Executive summary of this knowledge base. Two layers:

- `wiki/context/` is the identity layer: who this brain is and how it thinks. The
  human-reviewed source of truth. It wins when it disagrees with the knowledge base.
- `wiki/` is the knowledge base: interlinked facts (entities, concepts, sources,
  synthesis), built and maintained by the agent from what you put in `raw/`.

Fill in `wiki/context/` first (see the templates there), then ingest your sources.
